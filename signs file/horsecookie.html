Horse Sign

A CNC horse sign made out of a tree cookie and hand-painted offers a rustic, natural feel combined with artistic craftsmanship. The horse design is precision-cut using CNC technology, showcasing intricate details that capture the grace and beauty of the animal. The tree cookie serves as a unique, organic base, its natural rings and textures adding character. After cutting, the sign is carefully hand-painted, bringing vibrant colors and life to the piece. This sign would make a stunning, personalized addition to any home, ranch, or stable, blending nature with artistry in a truly one-of-a-kind way.
